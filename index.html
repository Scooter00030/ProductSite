Umphrey's McGee is an American progressive rock band originally from South Bend, Indiana. Although the band is part of the jam band scene, like Phish, and the Grateful Dead (with ever-changing setlists, improvisation, two sets per night, open taping policy, etc.) they are much more influenced musically by progressive rock artists such as King Crimson, Pink Floyd, Frank Zappa, Iron Maiden, Guns N' Roses, The Police, The Beatles and Led Zeppelin. UM experiments with many genres including rock, metal, funk, jazz, blues, electronic, bluegrass, and folk
